The Research and Consulting Center is a key unit of the university dedicated to promoting excellence in academic research and professional consulting. It serves as a platform for faculty members, researchers, and students to collaborate on impactful studies and real-world solutions. Established to bridge the gap between academia and industry, the center encourages interdisciplinary research and fosters innovation through various programs and strategic initiatives.
